# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

MPx Player should be a respectful, focused, and constructive project space.

Contributors, maintainers, and community members are expected to:

- communicate professionally
- assume good intent where reasonable
- give technical feedback clearly and respectfully
- keep disagreements focused on the work, not the person

## Unacceptable Behavior

This includes, but is not limited to:

- harassment or abusive language
- personal attacks
- discriminatory or exclusionary behavior
- deliberately disruptive conduct
- bad-faith collaboration

## Maintainer Responsibility

Maintainers are responsible for setting the tone of the project and may remove, edit, or reject contributions, comments, or interactions that violate this code of conduct.

## Reporting

If you experience or observe unacceptable behavior, report it privately to the project maintainer.

## Project Goal

The goal is simple: build excellent software in a space where people can contribute seriously and safely.
